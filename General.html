OUR STRATEGY

We are trying to save the feeling of original, but also giving it new colors

The results are going to be very interesting and useful to others. Our goal is simple - to make something old new. We understand the value and history of the original, but we also believe in the power of transformation and innovation. By combining the old with the new, we can create something unique and exciting that appeals to a wide range of people. Our hope is that our work inspires others to embrace both tradition and progress, and to reimagine what’s possible in their own lives and communities.

OUR DEBUT

“They Hunger” reborn on Source Engine.

1.

It’s been a long and winding road, but we’ve made it. After months of hard work and determination, we're proud to present “They Hunger” on the Source Engine for the first time ever.

2.

As huge fans of the series ourselves, we knew we had to do something to keep this masterpiece alive. And so, we set about porting “They Hunger” to the Source engine, taking the classic gameplay mechanics and updating the graphics and other elements to bring the game into the modern era.

They hunger: source

For those who don’t know, “They Hunger” is a classic survival horror series originally released in 1999 for the Half-Life engine. The game quickly gained a cult following for its combination of horror, humor, and survival elements, as players battled their way through hordes of zombies, giant mutant creatures, and other monstrous foes.

3.

Now, fans old and new can once again experience the thrills, chills, and laughs of “They Hunger” on a modern gaming system. We’re thrilled to present our debut project to the community, and we can’t wait to see what players think of our work. 

4.

So if you’re a fan of classic survival horror, or you just love a good, old-fashioned zombie romp, we invite you to check out “They Hunger” on the Source Engine. It’s been a labor of love for us, and we hope that shows in every aspect of the game.
